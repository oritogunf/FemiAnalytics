— Who We Are

A fellowship with roots, a family with reach.

GACSFEL FUNAAB has been a spiritual home for students passing through the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta — and the launchpad for an alumni body still walking, working, and worshipping together long after graduation.

Read our full story

Our Mission

To raise a generation of FUNAAB students rooted in the Word, filled with the Spirit, and sent into every sphere of influence.

Our Vision

A campus and a globe transformed by graduates whose lives bear the unmistakable fingerprint of Christ.

Our Foundation

Our motto says it all — Scripture, Prayer & Evangelism. Three pillars. One unshakable foundation in Christ.

Our Family

Past, present, and future — bound together by faith, mentorship, and a love that does not let go after graduation.
