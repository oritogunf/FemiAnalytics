01 Do I need any prior experience to join?
No prior experience is needed. Our Foundation phase starts from absolute basics: Excel, SQL fundamentals, and analytical thinking, and builds up gradually so total beginners can keep pace with career switchers.
02 Are the programmes live or self-paced?
Our programmes are cohort-based with live sessions twice a week, but every session is recorded. If you miss one, you can catch up on your own time without falling behind your cohort.
03 What kind of support do I get after graduating?
Career support including CV reviews, mock interviews, portfolio polishing, job referrals, and access to our alumni network, until you're hired. We don't disappear after the final session.
04 How long does the full programme take?
Most students complete the full track in 4–6 months depending on their pace and the path they choose. We offer flexible timelines so working professionals can balance learning with their day jobs.
05 Are there flexible payment options?
Yes. We offer split payments, monthly instalments, and limited scholarship slots each cohort. Talk to Isaac on WhatsApp to find a plan that works for your situation.
06 Will I get a certificate at the end?
Yes, you'll receive a Cephas IT certificate of completion, plus guidance on stacking industry-recognised certifications (Google, Microsoft, IBM) that strengthen your CV with real employers.
