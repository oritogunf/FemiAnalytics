FLATTENING
THE CURVE (COVID-19)

Nigeria is re-opening, but have we flattened the curve?

Over the past few days, I have invested time learning as much as possible about COVID - 19 in Nigeria. Recently I came across this trend FLATTENING THE CURVE from JOHNS HOPKINS UNIVERSITY RESOURCE CENTER and I decided to tell a story with my simple charts regarding the pandemic in Nigeria. WHAT IS FLATTENING THE CURVE? This is a public health strategy to slow down the spread of the SARS-CoV-2 virus during COVID -19 pandemic so that the peak number of people requiring care at a time is reduced, and the health care system does not exceed its capacity. This trend analysis show that if the measures placed by the government are strictly followed we could potentially see the curve flattening in few months' time. If not, the curve will continue to increase at its current pace.
